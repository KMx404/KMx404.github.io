Tips - Tutorial Purgatory

Published on 23 Jul 2020
How to escape the tutorial purgatory!

Welcome to KMx404's Tech World :D



What's a Tutorial Purgatory?


The Tutorial Purgatory is a state where you won't be able to get out of the tutorial learning curve. You'll always prefer watching videos rather than creating something yourself. You will never get satisfied, and never get enough tutorials to watch. Here in this article, I'm gonna give you few tips to help you get out of the Tutorial Purgatory.

Identify the problem


What do i mean here by identifying the problem?

You should be able to understand that this state you're stuck in is seriously bad and it won't help you in any way.

Along the way, you may need videos, tutorials, documentations to learn from but in the end its just a resource to take knowledege from not an occean to dive within.

The desperate urge to learn new things blinded you into jumping on tutorials, Left and right !

When you get stuck in such situations, You need to act Fast and find a way to break through ASAP.


Solve the issue


Now, we already know what the problem is. We need to solve it.


Jump on projects!


There are a lot of open source projects on github, You can easily contribute to one of them via Pull Requests or even join some organization. There are a lot of resources on the internet to learn from. Look for some projects that suits your skills or the Skills you want to gain. And Jump in



Don't focus much on the tutorials


As I said before, you may need to watch few tutorials along the way but it's not the main goal behind Programming.

Programming is the art of creation, Programming is a way of building your own stuff, Transforming those thoughts into a live action demo. Don't let the resources hold you down



Ask experienced programmers!


You don't need each slight detail you find on online courses or Classes. You need to be able to distinguish the things you need to focus on, and the things you need to leave behind. And there's no better way than asking the experienced programmers. The ones who walked on the same road.



Bonus: Learning new stuff won't hurt. but learn How to put this knowledge into something useful.
