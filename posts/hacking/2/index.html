Hacking steps


Hacking is basically the art of breaking things! This art have tools, techniques and also steps.


In this article i'm gonna walk you through the hacking process which you can put in action in order to hack someone!




Reconaissance:

This step consists of making a research.This phase is when you learn the needed skills in order to complete the hack

You should focus on the relevant skills to the type of hack you wish to emply, based on feasibilty determined by what

you find on the target

This phase is also known as the information gathering step and getting to know the target systems & status !


It's basically a set of processes and techniques (Footprinting, scanning & Enumeration) basically collecting anything


related to the victim or the target


OSINT (Open source intelligence)

is data collected from publicly available sources to used in an intelligence context

In order to find these kind of information you need to use specific tools enable you to acquire valuable data



Note: I'm not going to show you the details about these tools. You should look 'em up and try to use them


Maltego :

Maltego is an interactive data mining tool that renders directed graphs for link analysis.

The tool is used in online investigations for finding relationships between pieces of information

from various sources located on the Internet

Note: this tool is already installed in kali linux once you get it. So you better get kali because it'll offer you

to save much time and trouble


Shodan :

Google is the most used search engine for all whereas Shodan is a fantastic and goldmine search engine for hackers to see the exposed assets.

When compared to other search engines, Shodan provides you the results that make more sense and related to security professional


TheHarvester :

TheHarvester is an amazing tool for finding emails, subdomains, IP, etc. from various public data.


Recon-Ng :

Recon-Ng is an effective tool to perform reconnaissance on the target. The awesome part of this tool is the modular approach

The power of modular tools can be understood for those who used metasploit.

There are a lot of tools that can be used on this step! you can look them up and maybe see tutorials on the subject.


Surveillance :

Once you become skilled knowing all of the stuff that i've just told you about. Checking few tutorials & courses

Now, you lay on your deduction skills! In this phase you'll observe the target and notice each details he/she have

In this part you should know all of the common and uncommon things about your victim such as the financial status, Relationships status ..etc

You also configure your tools based on what you learn about the target.


Initiate interaction :

Knowing each details about your target can help you choose the best time/location/method you can use based on the information gathering part.

This is the first phase for you executing the hack. So you need to be careful ! Be comfortable, Be patient ..

This part can be hard specially at first so you need have patient in order for you to catch the final goal.

While being contacted by the target make sure, You have a plan in mind. So you can achieve your final desire without any distractions.

Note : You can should know few things about social engineering (Next article) in order to interact properly!


Maintaining Access :

Ensure you can make multiple interactions until you accomplish desired goal. This means you should build a strong relationship with the target!

BY making the target trust you . You can maintaing access so you can reach your final goal.


This part is when you show your affection. Here you need to look trust worthy and actually caring SO that you'll stay in touch. and the victim become vulunerable to you.


Clean UP :

After engagement with the target, perform cleanup to reduce risk of unintended consequences.

You need to wipe up everything. You shouldn't leave any trace that will lead to you that means :

NO personal data, Nothing related to your ordinary life. You need to be careful here!
